Singer-Songwriter

Photo by Adriana Correa

Marshall’s mother taught him how to sing at a very early age before a chronic illness robbed her of a truly beautiful singing voice. With her roots in rural Georgia, she taught her son how the right tune performed with the right spirit can cut as sharply as a surgeon’s scalpel. Marshall and his mother are still each other’s biggest fan.

Classically trained in orchestral upright bass, Marshall sought something more portable to accompany his voice and something more dynamic to help him when writing music. Over the years, he fell in love with learning guitar.
